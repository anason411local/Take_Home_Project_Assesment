Requirements & Warnings

Please carefully read the following requirements before uploading your dataset.

1
CSV Format Required

Please properly provide the dataset in CSV format only. Other formats (Excel, JSON, etc.) are not supported.

2
Required Column Order

Please properly follow the following column order:

date, daily_sales, product_category, marketing_spend, day_of_week, is_holiday
3
Computational Resource Warning

Don't upload too much years of historical data!
Our hosted machine or Docker container running this application is a Medium/Tiny instance. It doesn't have that much computational resources.
Recommended: 1-2 years of daily data maximum.

4
Model Auto-Selection

The latest models trained will automatically become the default models that the application will select for forecasting.

5
Tracking & Monitoring

Detailed tracking of model training can be seen in MLflow and Optuna Dashboard. Please click those dashboard buttons (top right corner) for final validation of training details, metrics, and hyperparameters.
