Understanding the Forecast
Forecast Line

The orange dashed line shows predicted future sales values based on the selected model's analysis of historical patterns.

Model Selection

Different models have different strengths. SARIMA excels at capturing trends, while XGBoost can capture complex patterns.

Limitations

Forecasts are predictions based on historical data. Actual results may vary due to unforeseen market conditions.

Confidence Intervals

The shaded area shows 95% confidence interval - there's a 95% probability that actual values will fall within this range.
